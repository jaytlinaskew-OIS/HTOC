My I&W Process—Other I&W authors may have a different process:

Scan ThreatConnect for indicators that have observations in two or more partners, sort by most recently seen or added.
If an indicator meets the requisite threshold, conduct research on the indicator in AlienVault OTX, VirusTotal, Mandiant, Pulsedive; and other sources as necessary. If an indicator has open ports listed on AlienVault OTX, check those ports using https://www.speedguide.net/port.php?port=8081 and do minor port characterization. If an indicator is linked to a state-sponsored, or other threat actor campaign, include brief research on the campaign in question and identify the actor in question. In the linked I&W example I include information concerning the indicator’s ties to Russian Foreign Intelligence Service (SVR) affiliated Midnight Blizzard.
Write narrative/fill in technical details as noted in the draft and the template.
Send report draft for review.
After review, convert Word doc to a PDF and upload to ThreatConnect, email to HTOC distro for dissemination.
